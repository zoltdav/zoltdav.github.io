Zsolt David is a writer from Hungary with an interest in Semiotics, Epistemology and Post-Structuralism. He has written about videogames for Kotaku UK, Fanbyte, Into The Spine, Unwinnable and Heterotopias. You may reach him on Twitter @zoltdav or by email at zsoltdavid(at)protonmail(dot)com about anything and everything. He's always interested in collaborating and are available for freelance writing and editing work. As an avid coffeine intaker, he rarely turns down an offer that keeps this circulation going.
